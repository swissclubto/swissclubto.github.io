Grüezi mitenand! :switzerland:

Interested in learning or practising Swiss German in a fun and relaxed way?
We're putting together a group interested in weekly online sessions (on your
own time!), plus a monthly in-person meetup to practise over a drink and good
conversation. :beers:

Whether you're just starting out, looking to improve, or a native speaker
wanting to refresh (or not lose!) your Schwiizerdütsch, you're very welcome to
join.

If this sounds like your thing, send an email to Céline and she'll share all
the details once we have an idea of numbers. There will be a course fee, which
will depend on the size of the group.

Let's learn, chat, and have some fun uf Schwiizerdütsch! :blush::switzerland:
